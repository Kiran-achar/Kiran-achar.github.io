Department of Computer Science Offers a coureses of B.C.A & B.Sc(Computer Science)

Carrier Opportunities

Computer science degrees pave the way for extensive job opportunities, Graduates with a bachelor's degree can work as software developers, database administrators, information security analysts, hardware engineers, or network architects. Some bachelor's graduates use their degree to qualify for master's programs, which allow for further advancement in the field. With the Computer Science specialization, one can apply for a range of computational jobs in the creative industries, business, finance, education, medicine, engineering and science. Typical job titles include application programmer, software engineer, creative coder, video game developer and systems analyst.

Eligibily Creteira for Admission

1. A candidate who passed the three year Diploma in the branch of computer science examination conducted by the board of Technical education, Government of Karnataka shall be eligible for admission to BCA degree course.
2. A candidate who passed the two year Pre University examination in science/commerce of Karnataka state or any other examination considered as equivalent are eligible for admission to the first semester of BCA degree course.
