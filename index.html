Chairperson's Message

“Education is the most powerful weapon, which can be used to change the world” – Nelson Mandela

Education is the greatest issue of our time, as striking the correct balance between the overall development of a youngster's personality and the competitive abilities of a future citizen of our country is a tremendous undertaking. I may say with pride and happiness that sahyadri Science college, Dept of Computer Science has been relentlessly committed to this endeavor since the past 15 years.

Our college's primary goal is to search & develop local talent among students, transforming them into competent individuals in all aspects of life. Ours is a college that develops new talent for entrance tests. Our goal is to prepare our students at the university level so that they can easily transition into the professional IT Jobs regime in the future. We also make certain that they are educated about life's greater principles and are aware of the world's socioeconomic realities. Their intellectual, logistical, competitive, and cultural qualities are all valued equally, and they are moulded into capable young people with great potential.

About Computer Science Department

Computer science degrees deal with the theoretical foundations of information and computation, taking a scientific and practical approach to computation and its applications. Computer science is considered by many of its practitioners to be a foundational science - one which makes other knowledge and achievements possible. The study of computer science involves systematically studying methodical processes (such as algorithms) in order to aid the acquisition, representation, processing, storage, communication of, and access to information. This is done by analyzing the feasibility, structure, expression and mechanization of these processes and how they relate to this information. Computer science department was stared in the year 1997 and BCA course started in year 2000. The Computer science department headed by Dr. Vidyashankar M.H and his team working hard to make students skill full with knowledge by providing training for skills and techniques along with new computer subjects. Many of students of B.Sc and BCA from Computer Science dept have got placement in companies like Infosys, Wipro, TCS, Accenture, Tech Mahindra etc. Computer Science department has conducted several special lectures by expertise resource persons and conducted Four workshops, and Four National Level conferences.
