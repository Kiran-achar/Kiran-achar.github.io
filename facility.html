Pathways and Library

The college is having an independent Library Building. It has three floors. The ground, first and second floors accommodates science college libraryand Arts & Commerce college libraries along with pathways training and placement cell.

The total constructed area of the building is 1522.47 sq. m. Following services are provided in the main library: Circulation Services, Bibliographical services, Current awareness Service, Internet facilities, Reprographic Service. Our college has the benefit of N-LIST on-line resource facility of UGC-inflibnet by which the faculty and students can access full articles of e-journals, e-books, and bibliography database, MathSciNet etc.


Cafeteria

The Sahyadri Science College has a well-maintained Cafeteria near to the main building. The college cafeteria has a constructed area of 266.2 sq. m. Snacks, soft drinks and vegetarian foods are available at reasonable prices for students, employees and visitors. Xerox & Internet service are available in the college cafeteria block.
